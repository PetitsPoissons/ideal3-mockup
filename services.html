ideal3 Claims Services

  • ideal3 is firmly committed to treating policyholders and claimants fairly. All our adjusters and claims attorneys are trained to:

  • Listen, be fair, be open, and execute the carrier's duty under the insurance contract in good faith.
  • Befamiliar and in compliance with those laws and regulations that impact claims in the appropriate state, and treat policyholders consistent with the requirements of the law.
  • Explain all relevant coveragges under the policy. Encourage policyholders to report all losses and avail themselves of benefits conferred under their coverages.
  • Diligently investigate the facts to determine if a claim is valid, reasonably evaluate the claim, and act promptly in resolving the claim. If it is necessary to reject a claim for coverage or damages, it should be done promptly and courteously, with an explanation for the decision.
  • Make an objective evaluation of the facts and circumstances supporting policyholders' claims.
  • Give insureds a reasonable opportunity to comply with their responsibilities under the policy. If a claim is rejected, be willing to listen to subsequent input from the insured. Complete any necessary follow up in a timely fashion, giving due consideration to any additional findings.
  • Communicate with and be responsive to inquiries from insureds by promptly responding to letters and phone calls.
