Terri Stough is an attorney with over a decade of experience in defense litigation and coverage. Terri counsels domestic, international, and surplus lines carriers regarding reserves, scope of policy coverage, reservations of rights, disclaimers, and declaratory judgments for all types of casualty, property, inland marine, contingency, general and professional liability policies. Prior to ideal3, Terri was in private practice representing clients in ccommercial litigation, product liability claims, personal injury, and replevin actions.


At ideal3, Terri handles complex claims involving catastrophic injuries, coverage disputes, excess exposure, and sports and entertainment programs. A Director of Complex Claims & Legal, Terri leads her team to handle claims with through legal analysis and exceptional investigative skills.

Natalie Kelly is a claims management professional with nearly two decades of experience in handling and managing a wide variety of claims, including Cyber Liability, Technology Errors and Omissions, General Liability, Professional Liability, Employment Practices Liability, Tenant Discrimination Liability, and Billing Errors & Omissions Legal Expense. Natalie has extensive experience with both open market and reinsurance claims. Prior to joining ideal3, Natalie managed a claims team of 25 claims professionals for a Lloyd's London coverholder with significant claims-handling authority.


As the Managing Director of ideal3, Natalie oversees and manages the operation of the company; develops, implements, and enforces policies and procedures; ensures compliance with internal controls and guidelines, fair-claims handling practices and claims management agreements; handles reporting to clients and reggulators; oversees quality assurance; and coordinates internal and external audits. Natalie also manages and mentors ideal3's claims adjusters and administrative team members.


Natalie is a licensed adjuster in all 34 states that issue adjuster licenses.
